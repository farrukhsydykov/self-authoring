### Introduction Guide

---

Our users each have their own experiences, circumstances, and needs. 

Some people benefit from doing the **Past Authoring** exercise first. 

One approach is to do:

1. **Present Authoring - Faults -** To sharpen your sense of self criticism

1. **Future Authoring -** To formulate a plan

1. **Present Authoring - Virtues -** To think about capitalizing on your strengths

1. **Past Authoring -** However, if you have significant issues in your past that are still causing you some trouble, you might consider starting with the **Past Authoring** exercise. 

You should do them in the order that you think will benefit you the most.
